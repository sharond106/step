My story.


I was born in Queens in NYC with my (older) twin sister and grew up with an older brother. My family moved to Pittsburgh, PA a few years later, and we still live there now! We live by a beautiful park that I like to walk, kayak, and ice skate at. I knew I wanted to study computer science in my senior year of high school, and after graduating from North Allegheny High School in 2018, I joined the Digital Media Design program at the University of Pennsylvania. In my free time, I like to cook, cross stitch, watch movies, and go on walks.
