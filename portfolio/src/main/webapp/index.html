About me.


I am currently a student in the class of 2022 at the University of Pennsylvania and found myself at the intersection between computer science and fine arts, studying in the Digital Media Design program at UPENN. At college, I was a teaching assistant for CIS 160, an introductory discrete math course for computer science students, and through this experience, I found a new interest in teaching! I joined a club that teaches local high schoolers basic web development skills and helps them create their own websites within a semester. Last summer, I was a camp instructor for a local summer camp, teaching the cutest middle schoolers introductory computer science topics, including game design, intro to python, and solving challenges with VEX IQ robots. I plan to start this fall as a teaching assistant for CIS 460/560, a computer graphics course open to undergraduate and masters students.

I live in Pittsburgh, PA. I was born in Queens in NYC with my (older) twin sister and grew up with an older brother. We live by a beautiful park that I enjoy to walk, kayak, and ice skate at with friends.

I love to cook, cross stitch, watch movies, and go on walks with my sister. I recently picked up on embroidery--it's a learning process.

I hope to spread joy to those around me and share my passions with the world.
